About smArt

Change the way you explore art

Conveniently search for artworks or art exhibitions that you find interesting in museums nearby.

What To Do?

Take a clear picture of a piece of art you are interested in and submit it.

We will provide information about the art piece and suggest museums or exhibitions hosting works by the same artist or the same art and style.

The museums location and details will be provided, as well as an option to buy tickets through our site.

Our features

Snap & Find

smArt includes features which makes it easier for you to search, find and visit.
Here are some of them. We are constantly improving so expect more features to be added in time.

AI integration

Provides fast and reliable search results through photo recognition technology.

Profile customization

Customize your profile to only receive museum and art exhibition recommendations based on your preferences.

Museums discounts

When buying tickets to museums or exhibitions through our app, you may be eligible for up to 20% discount.
