Typography

I chose Argos Nouveau typeface for the headings and title because it expresses perfectly the beauty and shapes of the Art Nouveau style.
For the body text I chose Groundy Old Style because it is easier to read than the Argos Nouveau. It also has the nice curved lines which we find in Art Nouveau style.

Logo sketch

The main idea of the logo was “A” which comes from “Art” and it was designed with curved lines so it expresses the style we find in Art Nouveau.
