Theme 01: Basic web

Individual submissions

Before creating the first responsive website, we've been introduced to Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML and CSS.

All of us had been given a style to use, create a moodboard and then introduce it through our responsive website. My given style was Art Nouveau, an international style of art, architecture and applied art, especially the decorative arts, that was most popular between 1890 and 1910. I tried my best to express the style which is based a lot on natural forms and structures, particularly the curved lines of plants and flowers.

    Our task was to redesign a website which belongs to a thalassotherapy centre situated in Irelands oldest holiday resorts, Kilkee. My main task in the group was to code the website, but I have also helped with the design process and communication.

    Kilkee Thalassotherapy Centre has won many awards and they deserved a redesign of their old website. Therefore we decided to keep it simple, but being a holiday resort we also tried to make the website more attractive and in theme with the place.
