Theme 03: Basic content

Tell a story withouth words

Both projects from Theme 03 were really fun to make. In this one we had to tell a story withouth words and we went with the idea that music can change your day in a beautiful way.

The main character bikes through a gloomy day in Copenhagen and happens to find some headphones at a lightstop. Even the gloomiest days can be changed with a bit of uplifting music.

    • Promoting an idea

    In this project our task was to promote the idea of respecting the biking rules in Copenhagen. We created a nice story in which a tourist arrives in Copenhagen and is taught how to respect the rules of biking while exploring the city in the best way possible - with a bike!
